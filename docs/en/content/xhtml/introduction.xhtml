Introduction

Trafficking in drugs and the precursor chemicals used in their illicit manufacture are among the most serious and frequently encountered of transnational crimes. The movement of these substances from sources of production to destinations for consumption generate significant illicit profits and threaten the health and welfare of people, families and societies. Many thousands of illicit drug users die tragically each year.

As a frontline law enforcement officer, you may encounter drugs and precursors being smuggled or trafficked. These cases need to be handled with a high degree of caution and professionalism.

You should carry this guide with you while you are on duty. It contains tools to address situations you may experience in the course of your duty.
