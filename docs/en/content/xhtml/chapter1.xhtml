1. Sources of Information

Information can be gathered from a variety of different sources. These can be divided into 5 categories:

Open Source:

  • TV/Radio
  • Print media
  • Internet

Workers:

  • Fishermen
  • Construction workers
  • Street vendors
  • Sex workers

Service Providers:

  • Taxi drivers
  • Bus operators
  • Social workers
  • Shipping agents
  • NGOs

Criminal Sources:

  • Informants
  • Suspects

Official Sources:

  • Officers from your agency
  • Other government agencies
  • International agencies
  • Neighboring Border Liaison Offices
