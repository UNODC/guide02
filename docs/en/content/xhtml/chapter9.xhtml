9. Technical Investigations

With lawful authority, the following technical surveillance options can provide crucial evidence and intelligence.



Video Surveillance
Video cameras allow you to collect visual evidence and intelligence. Images can be collected by:

  • Closed circuit television cameras (CCTV)
  • Cameras that can monitor the subject from afar, sending images remotely
  • Handheld cameras in observation posts, on foot or within a vehicle
  • Body-worn cameras to covertly record meetings
  • Mobile phone cameras used covertly



Listening Devices
Listening devices allow you to covertly monitor conversations. They can be installed in places where suspects will have discussions, including vehicles, vessels, premises and public places. The signal of the device can be picked up by radio or sent via phone lines.

Listening devices can be worn covertly by undercover officers and informants.



Phone Interception
Phone interceptions also allow you to covertly monitor conversations. Monitoring phone conversations usually requires special authority and liaison with the telecommunications provider.
